Danger Area Map

  • This map aims to visaulize geometric distribution of freeway collisions happend in 2017, Los Angeles.
  • Collisions are clustered by nearest intersected local road as red dot.
  • The larger the red dot means the more dangerous section.
  • To see the actual collsion count within 2017, please mouse on to the red dot. Our tooltips will show further details.
  • We also provide zoom option and map styles to feet user of different concern.
  • Users could also play with the dot filter with collision count and road attribute.
